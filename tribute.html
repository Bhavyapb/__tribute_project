Dr.Norman Borlaug

Dr.Norman Borlaug, 1970

  • born March 25, 1914, near Saude, Iowa, U.S.
  • died September 12, 2009, Dallas, Texas
Norman Ernest Borlaug, American agricultural scientist, plant pathologist, and winner of the Nobel Prize for Peace in 1970. Known as the “Father of the Green Revolution,” Borlaug helped lay the groundwork for agricultural technological advances that alleviated world hunger.
